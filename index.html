Haotian Zheng

 

I am currently a Ph.D. candidates in Biostatistics at the University of Pennsylvania under the supervision of Dr. Hongzhe Li . Before joining Penn, I completed my B.S. in Mathematics at Tsinghua University in China. I am passionate and interested in developing novel high-dimensional statistical and machine learning methods for Integrative analysis of real-world data. Specifically, my research focused on theoretical studies on transfer learning and its application to genetics and microbiome data.
