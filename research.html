Past Applied Projects

  • Food and Resulting Microbiota and Metabolite (FARMM) study
    Classification of longitudinal alterations in the abundance of fecal metabolites to specify metabolic functions contributed by the gut microbiome.

  • PennCHOP IGRAM study
    Association studies between human microbiome and longitudinal Body Mass Index (BMI) among infants and built a predictive model for the BMI using Random Forrest.

  • iFaM Plasma NMR Targeted metabolites study
    Analysis of relationship between longitudinal metabolites of different sources and diet for mice using Linear Mixed Models, considering living environment as a random effect.
